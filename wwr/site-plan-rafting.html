Overview

Purpose

This is a site plan which will provide the blueprint and guides from which to design and develop this project. It is also here to give you all the whitewater rafting information you'll need in order to book a trip that best fits the rafting experience you and your friends are looking for.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Water rafting enthusiasts such as the general public as well as schools, corporations, and clubs. What do they want? A place where they can easily access information about white water rafting. What do they expect to find? Vast information about the area of the white water rafting and about the sport as well for the new beginners.
